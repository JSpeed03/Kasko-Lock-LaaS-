Kasko Lock

The Financial Case for a Scalable Locker-as-a-Service (LaaS) Model

Executive Summary

The Kasko Lock system is an innovative, secure storage solution designed for high-traffic areas. Our model is simple: **Locker-as-a-Service (LaaS)** with a flexible, admin-controlled rental rate, currently set at **₱20.00 per use**. The system's core advantage lies in its **Master-Slave Architecture**, which centralizes high-cost components to dramatically reduce manufacturing costs, enabling a rapid, industry-leading Return on Investment (ROI) for operators.

The Kasko Lock Cost Advantage

Our architecture is the key to our low cost. We centralize all expensive components into one Master Unit, which then controls many low-cost Slave Units. This results in a **25.9% reduction** in Cost Per Locker (CPL) when scaling from prototype to production.

Cost Per Locker (CPL)

By scaling to a 50-locker cluster, our architecture reduces the average cost per locker by **25.9%** from the prototype phase, achieving a highly competitive production CPL of **₱1,560**.

50-Locker Cluster Cost

The total manufacturing cost for a 50-unit cluster is only **₱78,000**. The low-cost Slave units make up over 96% of the deployment, proving the model's efficiency.

Scenario 1: Conservative Payback (2 Uses/Day)

Even with a highly conservative estimate of just **2 uses per locker per day**, the daily revenue of **₱2,000** completely covers the **₱78,000** initial investment in only **39 days**.

Scenario 2: Peak Performance Payback (8 Uses/Day)

In a high-traffic location, a realistic peak rate of **8 uses per day** generates **₱8,000** in daily revenue. This results in an extraordinary full payback period of just **9.75 days**.

5-Year Revenue vs. OPEX

The system is designed for long-term reliability. The estimated 5-Year Operational Cost (OPEX) is a tiny fraction of the conservative revenue, ensuring high sustainable profit.

Target Gross Profit & Sales Structure

We apply a **50% gross markup** to the **₱78,000** manufacturing cost, resulting in a final B2B sales price of **₱117,000**. The operator still achieves an ROI in under 59 days.
